## The Appeal of Herbal Tea

Most herbal teas are caffeine-free, making them safe and enjoyable for everyone from children to the elderly. You can enjoy each herb's unique aroma and flavor while benefiting from their health properties.

## How to Brew Herbal Tea

### Basic Method
1. Warm your teapot with hot water
2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of dried herbs
3. Pour hot water (95-100°C / 200-212°F)
4. Cover and steep for 3-5 minutes
5. Strain into your cup

### Tips
- Flower and leaf herbs need about 3 minutes; root and seed herbs need 5+ minutes
- Keep the lid on to retain volatile beneficial compounds
- When blending, start with 2-3 varieties

## Recommended Herbs by Purpose

- **Relaxation**: Chamomile, Linden, Passionflower
- **Cold Prevention**: Echinacea, Elderflower
- **Digestive Aid**: Peppermint, Fennel
- **Skin Health**: Rosehip, Hibiscus
