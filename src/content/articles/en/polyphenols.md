## What Are Polyphenols?

Polyphenols are natural chemicals that plants produce to protect themselves from ultraviolet light and pests. They have strong antioxidant properties that help protect our bodies from free radicals.

## Major Types of Polyphenols

### Flavonoids
Flavonoids are the most common polyphenols, classified into flavonols, flavanones, and isoflavones. Green tea catechins and soy isoflavones are well-known examples.

### Tannins
Tannins are polyphenols with astringent properties, found abundantly in tea and wine. They have antibacterial and digestive-promoting effects.

### Anthocyanins
Anthocyanins are purple pigments found abundantly in blueberries and hibiscus. They are known for supporting eye health.

## Herbs and Polyphenols

Many medicinal herbs are rich in polyphenols. Drinking herbal tea regularly is a natural way to incorporate polyphenols into your daily routine.
