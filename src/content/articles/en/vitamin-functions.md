## What Are Vitamins?

Vitamins are organic compounds essential for our bodies to function properly. Since our bodies can barely synthesize them, we need to obtain them through our diet.

## Fat-Soluble Vitamins

Fat-soluble vitamins are absorbed along with dietary fats. Vitamins A, D, E, and K fall into this category. Because they accumulate in the body, excessive intake should be avoided.

- **Vitamin A**: Visual function, skin and mucous membrane health
- **Vitamin D**: Calcium absorption, bone health
- **Vitamin E**: Antioxidant protection, cell membrane integrity
- **Vitamin K**: Blood clotting, bone formation

## Water-Soluble Vitamins

Water-soluble vitamins include the B vitamins and vitamin C. They don't accumulate easily in the body, so daily intake is important.

Many herbal teas contain natural vitamin C — rosehip and hibiscus are particularly rich sources. Enjoying herbal tea daily is a natural way to supplement your vitamin intake.
