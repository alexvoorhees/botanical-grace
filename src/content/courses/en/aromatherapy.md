## Course Overview

The AEAJ (Aroma Environment Association of Japan) Aromatherapy Advisor qualification covers essential oil fundamentals for safe use in various settings. This course covers content for both the Grade 2 and Grade 1 Aromatherapy Certification exams.

Our school is an officially recognized AEAJ Aromatherapy Advisor teaching institution.

## Curriculum (8 Sessions / 16 Hours)

| Session | Course Content |
|:-------:|----------------|
| 1 | Aromatherapy Basics |
| 2 | Understanding Essential Oils |
| 3 | Safety and Precautions |
| 4 | Practical Aromatherapy Applications |
| 5 | How Aromatherapy Works |
| 6 | Aromatherapy for Beauty & Health Care |
| 7 | Exploring the History of Aromatherapy |
| 8 | Aromatherapy Laws and Regulations |

## About the Certification Exams

| Exam | Essential Oil Profiles | Scent Test |
|------|:----------------------:|:----------:|
| Grade 2 Aromatherapy | 11 varieties | 9 oils |
| Grade 1 Aromatherapy | 30 varieties | 17 oils |
