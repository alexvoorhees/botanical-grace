## Course Overview

The AEAJ (Aroma Environment Association of Japan) Aromatherapy Advisor qualification covers essential oil fundamentals and safe usage. Learn both theory and practice.

## Curriculum

- Aromatherapy Basics
- Essential Oil Fundamentals
- Safety and Precautions
- How Aromatherapy Works
- Practical Aromatherapy (Treatment Methods)
- Essential Oil Profiles
- Aromatherapy and Health
- Exam Preparation and Review

## Who This Course Is For

- Those interested in aromatherapy
- Those wanting to learn proper essential oil usage
- Those seeking AEAJ certification
- Those wanting to work as therapists
