## Course Overview

The Herb & Life Coordinator qualification helps you incorporate herbs and spices into cooking and daily living for a richer lifestyle.

## Curriculum

- Fundamentals of Herbs & Spices
- Cooking with Herbs & Spices
- Tea and Beverage Recipes
- Herbs for Beauty and Health
- Herbs for Living Spaces
- Practical Herb & Life Application

## Who This Course Is For

- Those wanting to use herbs and spices in cooking
- Those interested in a natural lifestyle
- Those seeking an introductory herb certification
