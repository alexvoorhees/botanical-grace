## Course Overview

The Herb & Life Coordinator course covers 56 herbs and spices — their scientific names, components, actions, applications, contraindications, and recommended usage. Learn to incorporate herbs into cooking, beauty routines, beverages, and even grow your own.

## Curriculum (6 Sessions / 12 Hours)

| Session | Course Content | Herbs & Spices (56 varieties) |
|:-------:|----------------|-------------------------------|
| 1 | Herb & Spice Fundamentals, History, Safety, Tea Basics, Aroma Baths | Oregano / Cardamom / Garlic / Clove / Coriander / Saffron / Cinnamon / Ginger / Star Anise |
| 2 | Cooking with Herbs & Spices, Brewing Techniques | Sage / Turmeric / Thyme / Tarragon / Dill / Nutmeg / Basil / Fennel / Pepper |
| 3 | Herbs for Beauty & Health I | Mint / Lavender / Lemongrass / Rosemary / Bay Leaf / Anise / Italian Parsley |
| 4 | Herbs for Beauty & Health II | Cumin / Capers / Sweet Marjoram / Chervil / Chives / Vanilla / Fenugreek / Mustard / Arugula |
| 5 | Growing Herbs & Spices | Gardenia / Ginger Lily / Japanese Pepper / Perilla / Horsetail / Dokudami / Job's Tears / Mugwort / Japanese Mint |
| 6 | Comprehensive Review & Exam Prep | Ashitaba / Egoma / Goji / Kudzu / Sesame / Seri / Tea / Chickweed / Loquat / Yuzu / Wasabi |
