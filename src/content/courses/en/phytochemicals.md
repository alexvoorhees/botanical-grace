## Course Overview

This plant therapy course covers the nutritional and phytochemical properties of vegetables, fruits, and herbs, as well as the actions of essential oils on the body, organized by organ system. Go beyond basic nutrition to explore polyphenols, carotenoids, and other plant chemical compounds in depth.

Graduates receive the "BOTANICAL THERAPY ADVISOR" credential.
