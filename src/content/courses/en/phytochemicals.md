## Course Overview

Phytochemicals are natural chemical compounds found in plants. This course covers the types and functions of phytochemicals and how to apply them to daily health management.

## Curriculum

- What Are Phytochemicals?
- Types and Functions of Polyphenols
- Types and Functions of Carotenoids
- Sulfur Compounds and Polysaccharides
- Diet Plans Using Phytochemicals
- Practical Plant Therapy

## Who This Course Is For

- Those interested in plant therapy
- Those wanting to improve health through diet
- Those wanting deeper knowledge of phytochemicals
