## Course Overview

The JAMHA (Japan Medicinal Herb Association) Certified Herbal Therapist is a professional qualification for medicinal herb specialists. This course covers 30 medicinal herbs and 12 essential oils in depth.

## Curriculum (18 Units / 36 Hours)

1. History and Future of Medicinal Herbs
2. Fundamentals of Medicinal Herbs I
3. Fundamentals of Medicinal Herbs II
4. Fundamentals of Aromatherapy
5. Safety of Medicinal Herbs and Essential Oils
6. Herbal Preparations I
7. Herbal Preparations II
8. Herbs That Support Metabolism
9. Lifestyle Improvement and Quality of Life
10. Herbs for Preventing Lifestyle Diseases
11. Herbs for Immune Defense
12. Herbs for Stress Management and Psychosomatic Prevention
13. Herbs for Women's Health
14. Herbs for Anti-Aging
15. Sensory Stimulation and Medicinal Herbs
16. Kitchen Pharmacy
17. Designing an Herbal Lifestyle
18. Enhancing Natural Healing Power

## Who This Course Is For

- Those seeking deep knowledge of medicinal herbs
- Those aiming for JAMHA Herbal Therapist certification
- Those wanting to incorporate herbs into their career
- Those interested in natural health management
