## Course Overview

The JAMHA (Japan Medicinal Herb Association) Certified Herbal Therapist is a professional qualification for medicinal herb specialists. This course covers 30 medicinal herbs and 12 essential oils in depth, from fundamentals through advanced professional knowledge.

Complete beginners are welcome. Both online and in-person classes are available.

## Who This Course Is For

- Health-conscious individuals wanting to incorporate natural remedies
- Parents who want to protect their family's health
- Medical and caregiving professionals seeking plant therapy expertise
- Those working in herb shops or aroma salons, or planning to open one
- Those aiming for JAMHA Herbal Therapist certification

## Career After Certification

After passing the certification exam and joining JAMHA, you receive official recognition. Certified therapists can open their own accredited teaching classroom from home. Career opportunities include herb shops, aroma salons, medical and care facilities, and more.
