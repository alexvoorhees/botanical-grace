Evening Primrose (Oenothera biennis) is a biennial plant in the Onagraceae family, native to North America. It gets its name from its beautiful yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. Native Americans have used this plant medicinally for centuries in their traditional healing practices.

The oil extracted from evening primrose seeds is rich in gamma-linolenic acid (GLA), an essential fatty acid involved in prostaglandin production that helps regulate hormonal balance. It is widely used to alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS) and menopause. The oil also possesses anti-inflammatory properties, making it valuable for addressing skin conditions such as atopic dermatitis and eczema.

In herbal therapy, evening primrose is primarily taken as an oil capsule supplement. It is regarded as an essential herb for supporting women's health concerns and maintaining healthy skin through natural care.
